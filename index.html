How To Make Potstickers

What you will need:

  • non-stick pan
  • cooking oil of choice
  • water
  • a standard-sized bowl
  • dumpling of choice

Cooking Steps:

  1. Heat the oil in the pan on medium-high heat
  2. Add the dumplings to the pan and cook until bottom is slightly golden;

    **BOTTOM MUST BE MOSTLY WHITE**

  3. Add enough water into the pan to cover the bottom of the dumplings (about a centimeter or two)
  4. Cover the pan with a lid and cook on medium-low heat until the dumplings are slightly translucent
  5. By now, there should be little to no water left in the pan. If there is, let the dumplings cook until there is no water left
  6. Remove the lid and serve with your condiment of choice!
